Who We Are?

Yuva Anand Sarvjan Kalyan Samiti is a not-for-profit NGO working in Bihar for 6 years,
focusing on youth,unemployment,education,social wealfare and social injustice in rular area.We do this through
well planned and comprehensive project in health, education, livelihoods and youth employment.
Our overall gols is the empowerment of people who live in rular leading in their lives and livelihoods.
